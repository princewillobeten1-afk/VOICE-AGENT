# Product Foundation

## Positioning

VoiceSense is the operating system for AI employees. It helps businesses build AI workers that can communicate, remember, act, and collaborate across customer-facing and internal workflows.

## Primary Users

- Founders and operators who want AI employees without deep implementation work.
- Agencies building AI automation for clients.
- Developers integrating AI employees into existing products.
- Enterprise teams that need governance, security, observability, and scale.

## Initial Product Areas

- AI employee builder.
- Conversation testing playground.
- Voice and chat channels.
- Tool and workflow configuration.
- Knowledge base connection.
- Logs, traces, analytics, and evaluations.
- Developer API and webhook management.

## UX Direction

The interface should be dense enough for serious work but calm enough to feel premium. Avoid marketing-style dashboards. Prioritize clarity, speed, and confidence.
