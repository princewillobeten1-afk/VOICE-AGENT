# Demo Workspace

The demo workspace is designed to let prospects and new customers understand VoiceSense quickly without connecting real systems.

## Demo Organization

- Organization: Acme Co
- AI Employee: Maya, Customer Support AI Employee
- Knowledge Base: Support policies, refunds, pricing, FAQs
- Workflow: Refund approval
- Integration: CRM sandbox
- Conversation: Billing dispute simulation
- AI Studio Scenario: Happy path, interruption, escalation, and policy exception tests

## Experience Goal

A first-time user should see a coherent operating system for AI employees within minutes: employee, prompt, knowledge, workflow, conversation, analytics, security, billing, and deployment readiness.
